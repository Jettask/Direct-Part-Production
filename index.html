WORLD-CLASS ADDITIVE EXPERIENCE

Direct Part Production is a digital manufacturer of advanced plastic and metal AM parts, backed production expertise. Equipped with one of the world’s largest global networks of industrial 3D printers and innovative digital solutions for aerospace, automotive, industrial, healthcare, defense, electronics, consumer products, and more, DPP supports projects from 1 part to more than 1 million. Leading in prototyping to production with international manufacturing, advanced technologies, and raw materials, DPP gets your product to market faster. With the choice of industrial equipment, custom software, quality of people, and the constant evolution of processes, we are passionate about everything that is required to make a modern manufacturing facility like this hum. From one of our very first projects of a rubber overmold for the Apple Newton to our continuing work with a distinguished group of companies and designers, our focus is always on the customer experience and the craftsmanship involved with bringing new products to life.
