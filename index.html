WORLD-CLASS ADDITIVE EXPERIENCE

Direct Part Production is a digital manufacturer of advanced plastic and metal AM parts, backed production expertise. We use a network of industrial 3D printers and innovative digital solutions for aerospace, automotive, consumer products, healthcare, and tooling.

DPP works in prototyping to production with international manufacturing, advanced technologies, and raw materials, DPP gets your product to market faster. With the choice of industrial equipment, custom software, quality of people, and the constant evolution of processes, we are passionate about everything that is required to make a modern manufacturing facility.

From one of our very first projects of a rubber overmold for the Apple Newton to our continuing work with a distinguished group of companies and designers, our focus is always on the customer experience and the craftsmanship involved with bringing new products to life.
